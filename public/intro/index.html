📋 How It Works

1
Start the bot
Open @Yangiliklar_Newsroom_Bot on Telegram and press /start. Choose your language.
2
Connect a channel
Add the bot as an admin to your Telegram channel. Enter the channel username (e.g., @my_channel).
3
Add sources
Add RSS feed URLs (news websites, blogs) or Telegram channels to monitor. The bot will automatically scan them.
4
Set interval
Choose how often the bot checks for new content (every 2-60 minutes). The bot handles the rest.
5
Relax
Your channel runs on autopilot. AI finds, summarizes, moderates, and publishes content 24/7.
